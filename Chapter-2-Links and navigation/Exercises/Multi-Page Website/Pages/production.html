Development

"The Lion king" making was intended to show how lions maintain the animal population by eating them, although in a mythological way. The other tale was during the discussion of making the film the topic of the story was set in Africa. That is how Jeffrey Katzenberg had the sudden idea. Where the perspective is the life of the animals such as their hardships, responsibilities and experience.

Animation

Through the hard and trials the artist did not have much faith when creating the "The Lion King", with the Chapman accepting the project even though the chance of popularity for the movie is low. The piece of the music was selected for its majestic and capturing lyrics, along with the strong beat. Releasing the movie, the accompany had earn $1 billion with the products based on the film.
