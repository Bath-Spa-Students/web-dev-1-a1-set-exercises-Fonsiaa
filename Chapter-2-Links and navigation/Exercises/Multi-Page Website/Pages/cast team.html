The Lion King

Voice Casts

The following are the very people who are only heard by audience playing the characters of 'The Lion King'. With such powerful and fitting voice they were selected to take the role and was able to add depth to the movie.

Matthew Broderick as Simba, who grows up to become the King of the Pride lands
Joseph Williams The adult singing voice of Simba
Jonathan Taylor Thomas The young singing voice of Simba
Jeremy Iron as Scar, King Mufasa's younger brother
James Earl Jones as King Mufasa
Moira Kelly Nala, Simba's best friend
Zoe leader as Sarafina, Nala's loving mother
Sally Dworksy The singing voice of Nala
Nathan Lane as Timon, a self-absorbed yet loyal friend meerkat to Simba
Ernie Sabelle as Pumbaa, a comedic and selfless warth. A bestfriend of Simba and Timon
Robert Guillaume as Rafiki, an elderly shaman mandrill who serves the Pride Lands
Rowan Atkinson as Zazu, a hornbill serving King Mufasa
Madge Sinclair as Queen Sarabi, Mufasa' love partner and Simba's mother
Whoopi Goldberg as the female leader of the hyena clan
Cheech Marin as Banzai, the impulsive and always complaining hyena
Jim Cummings as the laughing communicator hyena and singing voice of Scar
